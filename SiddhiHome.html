Hotel in Bhaktapur - Siddhi Home & Nyatapola Rooftop Cafe

Offering a terrace and views of the city, Siddhi Home & Restaurant is located in Bhaktapur, 200 metres from Bhaktapur Durbar Square. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant.

Certain rooms feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom equipped with a shower. For your comfort, you will find slippers and free toiletries. Siddhi Home & Restaurant features free WiFi throughout the property. A flat-screen TV with cable channels is available.

There is a 24-hour front desk at the property.

The guest house also offers bike hire and car hire. The nearest airport is Tribhuvan Airport, 7 km from the proper
